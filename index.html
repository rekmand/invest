Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Mutual Fund?

A mutual fund is a professionally managed investment fund that pools money from many investors to purchase securities. This allows investors to diversify their portfolio and gain access to professional management at a lower cost than they could on their own.

How do I start investing with Rekmand?

Starting your investment journey with Rekmand is simple. You can explore our services, use our investment tools, and then contact us directly through the form on this page or by phone/email to schedule a consultation. We'll guide you through every step.

What are the risks involved in mutual fund investments?

All mutual fund investments are subject to market risks. The value of investments may go down or up depending on the factors and forces affecting the securities market. It's important to read all scheme related documents carefully. Our risk profiler tool can help you understand your risk tolerance.

Do you offer personalized financial planning?

Yes, we offer comprehensive and personalized financial planning services. Our advisors work closely with you to understand your financial goals, risk tolerance, and current situation to create a tailored plan that helps you achieve your objectives.
